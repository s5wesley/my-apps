Mission and Context

LAFABAH Houston (La Famille Bamiléké of Houston) is a long-standing cultural and civic institution founded in 1986. The association has structured leadership, sustained community engagement, and a clear intergenerational focus.

This first phase centralizes communication of who LAFABAH is, what it does today, and where it is heading through NextGen and Project Polaris.

Structured Governance

LAFABAH operates with established leadership and accountability processes that support trust, continuity, and decision-making.

  • Formal executive structure and role clarity
  • Consistent association communication
  • Transparent long-term planning principles

Credibility Signals in Phase 1
